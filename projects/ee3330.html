EE 3330 Final Project: UAV Flight Controller

Dec 2025

Designed and implemented a custom UAV flight controller integrating STM32H7 MCU, IMU, barometer, and USB-C power/data interface. The controller processes sensor data in real-time to drive ESCs and motors.
