MATLAB Student Ambassador

MathWorks · Jan 2025 – Present

As a MATLAB Student Ambassador, I serve as a technical and outreach liaison between MathWorks and Iowa State University. My role focuses on helping students effectively use MATLAB and Simulink for coursework, projects, and research while promoting best practices in technical computing.

Responsibilities

I organize and lead workshops, classroom demos, and help sessions that introduce students to MATLAB workflows relevant to engineering coursework. I also provide one-on-one support for debugging code, improving performance, and understanding numerical and simulation-based concepts.

Technical Skills Applied

This role strengthened my ability to communicate technical ideas clearly while reinforcing my own understanding of numerical methods, data analysis, visualization, and simulation. I regularly work with MATLAB scripts, functions, plots, and Simulink models to support real academic use cases.

Impact

Through targeted outreach and peer support, I help increase MATLAB adoption across campus and enable students to work more efficiently on labs, projects, and research. Acting as a bridge between students and MathWorks has given me valuable experience in technical communication and mentorship.

Outreach & Media

I used Instagram to promote workshops, share MATLAB tips, and highlight student engagement. This platform helped extend outreach beyond in-person events and reach a broader student audience.
